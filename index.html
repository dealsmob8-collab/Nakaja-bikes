Troubleshooting Quick Fixes

Vehicle not starting

  • Check ignition switch
  • Ensure kill switch is ON
  • Check spark plug connection
  • Check fuel tank vent hole clogging

High fuel consumption

  • Check tire pressure
  • Inspect for fuel leakage
  • Clean dirty air filter

Break-in period

During first 1,000km keep speed under 65km/h and avoid too much full throttle.

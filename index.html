  • Daily checks: fuel, leaks, oil level

Quick Checks on the Road

Bike Won’t Start

  • Check ignition switch
  • Check kill switch
  • Check spark plug connection
  • Check fuel tank vent hole

Using Too Much Fuel

  • Check tire pressure
  • Inspect for fuel leakage
  • Clean dirty air filter

First 1,000km

During the first 1,000km, keep speed under 65km/h and avoid too much full throttle.
